## Project Overview
#### The SeSameOS3 project primarily addresses the Bluetooth connection of hardware devices such as Sesame 5, Sesame 5 Pro, Sesame Bike2, Sesame BLE Connector1, Sesame Open Sensor, Sesame Touch 1 Pro, Sesame Touch 1, and WIFI Module2. It helps users intelligently operate hardware through the Android mobile application. If your product is Sesame Bot 1, Sesame 3, Sesame 4, or Sesame Bike 1, please download [SesameSDK_Android_with_DemoApp](https://github.com/CANDY-HOUSE/SesameSDK_Android_with_DemoApp)
